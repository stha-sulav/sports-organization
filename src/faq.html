Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered. Find out more about Campus Champs here.

What is Campus Champs?

Campus Champs is a dynamic university sports organization dedicated to fostering athletic talent, promoting teamwork, and encouraging a healthy lifestyle among students.

How can I join Campus Champs?

You can join Campus Champs by visiting our membership page, where you'll find all the information you need to get started.

Do I need to be a student to participate?

Yes, Campus Champs is exclusively for university students who wish to participate in sports, compete in tournaments, or be part of our athletic community.

What sports are available at Campus Champs?

We offer a variety of sports including football, basketball, swimming, tennis, and chess. We also hold annual tournaments across these sports.

How can I contact Campus Champs?

You can contact us through our Contact Us page or directly email us at contact@campuschamps.org.
